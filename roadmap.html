Future

🎮 The Guardian Game

  • Development of a Cubyboo game — protect essences against the Noir
  • Playable directly on the Prismatic Sanctuary or as a standalone app
  • NFT utility in-game — your Cubyboo and its essence define your role
  • Community-driven game design — Guardians vote on mechanics
  • Seasonal events tied to real-world dates and community milestones

🌐 Ecosystem Expansion

  • Second Cubyboo collection — new essences, new stories, same universe
  • Cubyboo Lore Universe — expanded written and visual lore by the community
  • Guardian DAO — full community governance of the project treasury
  • Physical Cubyboo — limited edition plush toys for top Guardians

🤝 Partnerships & Collaborations

  • Major NFT collection collaborations — cross-universe guardian events
  • Brand partnerships aligned with Cubyboo values — nature, art, wellbeing
  • Potential gallery exhibitions — Cubyboo as fine digital art
  • Educational partnerships — Cubyboo as a tool for mindfulness
