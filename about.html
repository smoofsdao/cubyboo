The Origin

Birth of the First Bear

Long ago, our world blazed with infinite color. Every hue vibrated with its own energy. Elements danced in perfect harmony. Human emotions painted every moment with infinite nuance. The world was a perfect prism where every essence played its part in the great symphony of existence.

Then, without warning, things began to fade. The sages uncovered a terrifying truth: every essence of the world can only exist if it is cherished and protected. Without a guardian to defend it, it slowly erases itself from reality — like a memory dissolving into forgetting. This invisible decay became known as the Noir.

« The Noir is not a creature. It is not a demon. The Noir is the reflection of our own collective indifference. »

In one last act of hope, the Universe answered. From the heart of the Primal Prism emerged the First Bear — the perfect archetype of the protector. Gentle yet brave. Cute yet powerful. Accessible yet sacred. From the First Bear were born the 2 222 Cubyboo — each carrying the same sacred form, each sealed with a unique invisible essence awaiting its Co-Guardian.

The Cubyboo do not move. They have sworn to remain eternally still — an absolute discipline, a permanent cosmic meditation that keeps their essence anchored in reality. They cannot fight the Noir by force. The only way to defeat it is to exist, to be seen, to be cherished.

« As long as a Cubyboo is cherished by its human Guardian, what it protects can never disappear. »
